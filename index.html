General information about the library

The Central Library was established in 1989 on its site in the Jadiriyah complex Its objectives are to serve the beneficiaries of the university's professors, students and employees. Among its holdings are all the sources of information in the scientific, humanitarian and social fields, and since its founding it has been keen on providing scientific research and providing services to the beneficiaries.

    2. The Iraqi Virtual Scientific Library: Involvement of the Central Library with the Ministry of Higher Education for the purpose of benefiting professors and postgraduate students and the stages of the preliminary studies.
    3. Issuing the identity of the central library of professors and students at the university
    4. Library of the Central Library
    5. Lending: It involves lending books, papers, projects, periodicals and other information vessels
